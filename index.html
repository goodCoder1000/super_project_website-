Building

Building in Minecraft is all about creativity! You can start with simple shelters and eventually create giant castles, farms, or even whole cities. The key is to gather the right blocks, plan out your structure, and keep experimenting with new ideas. Whether you're building for survival or just for fun, the world is your canvas!

Best Blocks to Build With

  • Spruce Planks – Great for houses, cabins, and anything rustic.
  • Stone Bricks – Perfect for castles, walls, and strong structures.
  • Quartz Blocks – Clean and modern for futuristic or luxury builds.
  • Dark Oak Logs – Adds depth and strong outlines to builds. Looks like wet wood.
  • Glass (Any Color) – Essential for windows, skylights, and decorative accents.

Tips for Building

  1. Use block gradients (you can search them online)
  2. You can use logs as supports!
  3. Add lanterns because you need to light up your builds and they look cool :)
  4. Add depth to your builds.
  5. Plan your designs beforehand.
  6. Use a variety of different styles

PvP

PvP is all about timing, movement, and knowing your gear. Mastering strafing, hit cooldowns, and quick item switching will greatly improve your fights.

  • Time your hits (don't spam in 1.9+).
  • Strafe and jump to break enemy aim.
  • Keep healing items and pearls in easy slots.

Mining

Mining is the main way to gather ores, resources, and materials. Good tools, smart planning, and safe tunnels help you collect more while avoiding danger.

  • Always bring torches, food, and extra pickaxes.
  • Mine at Y-levels that match the ore you want.
  • Use strip-mining or branch-mining for efficiency.
